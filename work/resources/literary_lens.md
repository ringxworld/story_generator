# Literary Lens

Use this checklist when reviewing reference outputs.

## Structure

- What is the chapter objective?
- What changes from opening state to closing state?
- What unanswered thread is introduced or escalated?

## Character

- Which character drives decisions in this chapter?
- What motivation is explicitly stated vs implied?
- Which relationships gain or lose trust?

## Dialogue

- Where does dialogue carry conflict instead of exposition?
- Are there recurring speech patterns that define voice?
- Which lines reveal power dynamics or hidden intent?

## Pacing

- Where does the chapter slow down for emotional beats?
- Where does it accelerate for tension?
- Is the transition between reflection and action smooth?

## Transfer to your story

- Which technique is reusable in your setting?
- Which technique should be avoided for originality?
- What concrete rule should be added to your story spec?
